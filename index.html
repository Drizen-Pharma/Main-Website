About DRIZEN PHARMACEUTICALS LLP

Established on **September 9, 2025** (as per Certificate of Incorporation **LLP Identification Number: ACR-1888**), DRIZEN PHARMACEUTICALS LLP is committed to providing innovative and effective healthcare solutions. Our core philosophy, **"Rooted in Science. Committed to Care,"** guides our every decision, from research and development to distribution.

We are a wholesale distributor of drugs, holding essential licenses including **Form 20B (GJ-SUR-256810)** for general drugs and **Form 21B (GJ-SUR-256811)** for Schedule C and C(1) drugs, ensuring full compliance with the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940. Our operations are based in **Surat, Gujarat, India** (Shop No. 302, Gruham Empire, Sayan Road, Kosad, Amroli).

Our team is dedicated to rigorous quality control and ethical practices, aiming to contribute positively to public health across India.
